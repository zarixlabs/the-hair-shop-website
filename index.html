Our Story

A Passion for Beautiful Hair

Nestled at Shop 1/12 Melton Street, Biloela, The Hair Shop is your local destination for world-class hair services. We believe that a great haircut is more than just a service — it's an experience that leaves you feeling confident, refreshed, and truly yourself.


Our team of passionate stylists bring years of experience, ongoing education and a genuine love for their craft to every appointment. From precision cuts to creative colour, we use only the finest professional-grade products to ensure results that exceed your expectations every single time.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to The Hair Shop.

We strongly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time, especially on Fridays and Saturdays. Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability. Call us on +61 7 4992 3762 to check availability or use our booking form above.
We offer a full range of colouring services including full tints, root touch-ups, foil highlights, balayage, ombre, toning and colour corrections. Our specialist colourists will assess your hair and create a personalised colour plan just for you.
Absolutely! Curly and wavy hair is one of our specialties. We use curl-specific cutting techniques and products that enhance your natural curl pattern rather than fighting it. Bring reference photos and let's create something beautiful together.
We use and retail a curated range of professional-grade hair care products including shampoos, conditioners, treatments, styling products and colour care. Our team will recommend the right products to maintain your results at home.
Timing varies depending on the service. A root touch-up typically takes 1–1.5 hours, full foil highlights 2–3 hours, and balayage up to 3–4 hours. We always factor in processing time, a treatment and a blowout in your appointment.
Yes! There is ample street parking available on Melton Street directly outside the salon. We're conveniently located near Drakes Biloela and the Commercial Hotel, making us very easy to find in the centre of Biloela.
Yes, we love having little ones in the salon! We offer children's cuts in a relaxed, friendly environment. Our stylists are experienced at working with kids to ensure the experience is fun and comfortable for everyone.
We're open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays. We recommend calling ahead to book your appointment to ensure we can accommodate your preferred time.
